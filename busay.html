Busay

Cave Busay is a nice and not so difficult short adventure for thrill-seekers. The cave offers an easy but exciting walk inside. The cave is located in Barangay Busay, Moalboal Municipality, Cebu, Philippines. It is in the southwestern part of the province where you can find lots of nice beaches, secluded islands and pristine seawater.
