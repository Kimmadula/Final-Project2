Cabana

Cabana Beach Club Resort in Moalboal is nestled snugly on a thousand square meter seafront lot of pristine waters. Cabana is a homey beach resort that arrests the ambiance of an Asiatic coastal paradise. Exotic and enclosed to secure the maximum of serene privacy, Cabana Beach Resort in Moalboal is a gateway to a double unforgettable adventure: at sea – great diving spots, rated among the best in the world, on land – international human interaction in bars, shops and lounges rimming the shoreline.
