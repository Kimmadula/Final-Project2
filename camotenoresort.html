Bukilat Cave

One of the island’s wonders that contributed to its rise to fame is Bukilat Cave, an enchanting cave with cold and pure waters one can swim in.

Paraiso Cave

Paraiso Cave is the most developed of the caves on Camotes Island, with a restaurant and resort having been built around it.

Timubo Cave

Timubo Cave Resort is almost like a hole or entrance of a bunker in the ground. The opening is just more than enough for humans to enter in. To finally enter into the clear natural pool, you have to pass through a sort of “eye of the needle”, so watch your head!
