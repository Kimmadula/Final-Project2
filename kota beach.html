Kota Beach Resort

Kota Beach is a modern native-inspired resort with 20 bungalow cottages, 15 side-by-side rooms, and its Kadlawon Restobar. Its location directly faces the golden sunrise. The service is friendly and paramount. Wake up to a golden sun, swim in turquoise seawater, eat local food in the open-air resto-bar, explore the town just a walk away, have a twilight afternoon meditation, sleep in your native room.
