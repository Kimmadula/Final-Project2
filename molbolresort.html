Basdaku

In Moalboal, Cebu Province, there is a popular one-kilometer long stretch of white sand beach - Basdaku Beach Resort! Along the coastline of this beach you’ll find various sorts of establishments and facilities that cater to guests' needs who come to enjoy the beach and the sea.

Panagsama Beach

Moalboal's Panagsama Beach is where most of the resorts, restaurants and dive shops are located. Most visitors to Moalboal will find themselves here.

Cabana

The car parking and the Wi-Fi are always free, so you can stay in touch and come and go as you please. Restaurant and outdoor pool are among the special facilities that will enhance your stay with on-site convenience.
