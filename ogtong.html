Ogtong Cave

Ogtong Cave Resort is one of the most visited and interesting places to see in Bantayan Island, Cebu Province, Philippines. If you have already seen one or two beaches in Santa Fe, Bantayan you can expect that this resort would also be as great. The cave is available for swimming or dipping. It is pure and cool and particularly refreshing in this tropical temperature.
