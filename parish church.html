St. Peter and Paul Parish

Bantayan Island is the seat of the first and therefore oldest Parish in Visayas and Mindanao founded in 1580. The original church was built under Spanish rule and was used as a first stop for priests entering the country. Here they would learn the language and culture before being sent out to various parts of the Philippines. The church also houses a small museum, visiting by appointment. Or if there is someone in the office when you are there they can let you in.
