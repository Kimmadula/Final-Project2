Taginis Falls in Moalboal are one of Cebuâ€™s secrets. Still largely unheard of, Taginis Falls and Budlot Spring are starting to generate interest thanks to word-of-mouth visitors. They may not be the tallest like Mantayupan, as impressive as Kawasan, or gushing with tonnes of water, but they are a lot of fun. For a bit of adventure, and to visit somewhere unique, Taginis Falls is one of the must-see waterfalls in Cebu. A visit to Taginis Falls is like going to a natural waterpark, complete with a couple of slides and jumps.
