Timubo Cave

Located in the middle of a farm in Camotes Islands, Cebu, Philippines. It cannot be easily found since it is not the same as most caves that you can enter through its visible and above the ground. Timubo Cave Resort is almost like a hole or entrance of bunker in the ground. The opening is just more than enough for humans to enter in. To finally enter into the clear natural pool, you have to pass through a sort or “eye of the needle”, so watch your head!
