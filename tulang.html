Tulang Diot

Tulang Diot has a light house, but that’s not what makes it special. It is the serenity and rustic atmosphere that make it stand out among other places on the island of Camotes. If you were looking for a place to be alone and away from the ‘noise’ of the busy world, this bucolic and countryside beach could be the place. Another unique feature of Tulang Diot is its beach. It has an islet beach that appears during low tide and gets submerged during high tide - a sandbar.
